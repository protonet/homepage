Protonet Demo
 
At demo.protonet.info you'll find a demo of the protonet software. You can test some functions as a logged out user (we call them strangers) or register to access all features. As a registered user you can add channels, chat with other users, create folder, upload data, publish data and much more. Contact us for an overview of available administrative features.

You will find us on demo.protonet.info, so feel free to start a private chat with Ali, Christopher, Henning or Wolfgang to get answers to all your questions.
 
Pricing Model
 
First, thank you all for your participation in our survey in February.
To guarantee the best possible support and to best incorporate your product wishes we decided on a renting model. With a monthly fee of EUR 149,-* you'll get support, regular software updates with new features as well as hardware monitoring, the protonet hardware for your office is obviously included. The contract runs for 12 months and can then be cancelled on a monthly basis. Set up fee is EUR 599,-*.
*Prices plus tax, there are of course no changes for present customers.
