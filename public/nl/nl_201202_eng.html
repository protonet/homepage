Dear protonet-Fans,

your waiting has come to an end, our team is on the home stretch. The protonet website is almost finished. We are pleased about the amount of interest towards protonet even before the official launch. We want to thank you for the interest and take this opportunity to introduce you to the product and the team.


Protonet is an easy, secure cloud solution for small and medium sized enterprises. We develop an operating system that comprises a router, fileserver and collaboration platform all in one device. Until now there has been no hardware solution to this, but we deliver all this with the protonet node.

We offer
- an intelligent one-button it-infrastructure,
- easy data exchange with co-workers and customers,
- online collaboration through a social interface and
- secure management of sensitive data.

In order to configure the node as close to your requirements as possible, we've created a quick survey. We would appreciate it if you have a couple of minutes to answer the four questions. It will help us out a lot :)

There are already six of us. We introduced ourselves earlier this month in our blog. This is the short version: Two ex-Xing software architects, one ex-Hanse Ventures business analyst, one serial web-entrepreneur, one industrial designer and artist and one architect and coder.

Our Website will go live within the next few weeks. Most of the time we are hard at work getting our product ready for open-beta, nevertheless you can rest assured that we'll update you by beginning of march!
